### 编译
```shell
wget -O- "https://c-ares.haxx.se/download/c-ares-1.15.0.tar.gz" | tar xz
cd c-ares-1.15.0
./configure --prefix=/root/x64
make install-strip -j $(grep "cpu cores" /proc/cpuinfo | wc -l)
cd ..
rm -rf c-ares-1.15.0
```
